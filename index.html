Legal

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025

Short version: Shape Rush does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We don't track you. We don't sell your information. We don't even have a server to send your data to.

Data collection

Shape Rush does not collect any personal data whatsoever. We do not collect your name, email address, device identifiers, location, usage analytics, crash reports, or any other information about you or your device.

Game Center

Shape Rush uses Apple Game Center for global leaderboards. When you choose to sign in to Game Center, Apple handles all authentication and score submissions directly. We do not receive, store, or have access to your Apple ID, Game Center profile, or any personal information. Game Center use is entirely optional — you can play Shape Rush without signing in. Apple's own privacy policy governs your Game Center data. You can review it at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Third-party services

Shape Rush does not include any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or tracking libraries. The only optional third-party integration is Apple Game Center, as described above.

Changes to this policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version here with a new effective date. Any significant changes will also be noted in the App Store release notes.

Contact

Have a question about privacy? You can reach us through the App Store support link on the Shape Rush app page.
